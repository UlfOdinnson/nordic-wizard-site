About

This is not a store. It is a record of becoming.

This did not start with skill.

It started with curiosity.

In 2022, I made my first piece after buying an axe and following a tutorial. Cheap leather, basic tools, and no real understanding of what I was doing. Just trying to make something work.

I did not know what different types of leather were. I did not know how materials behaved. I did not know what good work looked like.

I just kept going.

I bought leather I did not understand. Some of it waxy, some of it soft, some of it fought me every step of the way. I made mistakes constantly. Bad cuts, uneven stitching, pieces that did not come out the way I imagined.

But every piece taught me something.

Over time, I started working with better materials. Leather that demanded more control and more attention. There was no hiding flaws. If something was off, it showed.

This site is not a store.

It is a record.

Every year, every piece, every mistake and improvement lives here. This is the path from beginner to craftsman.

And I am still walking it.
