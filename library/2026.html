This is where I am now.

Still learning, still experimenting, but with a clearer understanding of the craft than ever before.

The focus has shifted toward refinement. Small details. Precision. Pushing materials further. Exploring design in a way that feels both controlled and expressive.

Every piece now carries everything that came before it. The early mistakes, the repetition, the experiments, all of it shows up in the work.

Keep getting better.
