2025

Where it expanded.

A year of pushing scale and detail.

I began working with metallic vegetable-tanned leather, adding a new dimension to my pieces. Hand-punched decorative work became part of my designs, bringing more depth and character.

This year also brought meaningful connections. Through trade and collaboration, I met Mikeal from Full Heart Forge. What started as a trade became a friendship.

Commissions grew in size and ambition. The iPad cover became the largest piece I had made at the time, fully stitched with my overcross technique.

I ended the year returning to something personal. Crafting gifts for family. Covering my Barry King maul in leather, turning even my tools into something that carries my mark.

This year was about scale, detail, and connection.
